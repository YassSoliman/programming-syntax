The "Unknown:"s below indicate that an entry is incomplete.

  • either the entry exist in the language, and please tell.
  • either the entry doesn't exist in the language, and please tell so. The entry will be marked as such and won't appear as missing anymore.
  • Various

    { ... }block (grouping statements, especially when statements are not expressions)
    #commenting (until end of line)

  • Control Flow

    bbreaking control flow (goto (unconditional jump))
    t labelif_then
    ;sequence
    end-of-linesequence

  • Strings

    s/.{n}(.).*/\1/accessing n-th character
    s/.{n}(.{len}).*/\1/extract a substring
    p or isimple print (on strings)
    \U / \L / \Cupper / lower case character

  • Bags and Lists

    /elt/is an element in the list
